About Appliance Touch

Appliance Touch is a trusted, family-owned electrical and appliance service company serving Sydney and surrounding suburbs. With over 8 years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and outstanding customer service.

Our team of licensed electricians and certified appliance technicians are committed to providing high-quality workmanship at competitive prices. We understand that electrical and appliance issues can be stressful, which is why we prioritize clear communication, punctuality, and getting the job done right the first time.

Whether you need a simple repair, a complete installation, or emergency service, Appliance Touch is here to help. We take pride in our work and stand behind every service we provide with our satisfaction guarantee.
